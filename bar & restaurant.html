Beachclub Far Out

Cuisine: Grill, Internationaal

Nestled along the picturesque coastline of Zandvoort, Beachclub Far Out is a true oasis by the sea. With its laid-back atmosphere and stunning views of the North Sea, this beachfront restaurant offers a perfect setting for relaxation. Guests can savor a diverse menu featuring fresh seafood, Mediterranean cuisine, and refreshing cocktails while soaking in the sun on the expansive terrace. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner or a casual beachside lunch, Beachclub Far Out is a top choice.

Thalassa:

Cuisine: European

Located near the heart of Zandvoort, Thalassa is a welcoming Greek restaurant known for its warm hospitality and authentic Mediterranean flavors. Hippie Fish:

Cuisines: Dutch, Seafood, European

Hippie Fish is a bohemian-inspired seafood restaurant that captures the free-spirited essence of the 1960s. De Haven van Zandvoort:

Cuisine: Dutch, Bar, Fish and Seafood, European

De Haven van Zandvoort, which translates to "The Harbor of Zandvoort," is a charming restaurant located in the historic harbor area of the town. With its nautical-themed decor and waterside location, it provides a tranquil escape from the bustling beach scene. The menu features a mix of Dutch and international cuisine, with an emphasis on fresh seafood. Diners can enjoy their meals while watching boats come and go in the harbor, making it a delightful spot for a relaxed dining experience with a touch of maritime charm
