  • Name: Hoek tot Helder (windraam)
  • Location: Strand van Zandvoort

"Hoek tot Helder" stands as the world's longest kitesurf journey, a challenging adventure that spans a remarkable 130 kilometers in a single day along the tumultuous North Sea. This epic event has earned its place as a must-do experience for kitesurfers from Europe and around the globe, not only delivering an unparalleled adrenaline rush but also fostering a sense of community among participants who share a passion for this exhilarating water sport. The challenging conditions and unpredictable nature of the North Sea make "Hoek tot Helder" a testament to kitesurfing excellence, pushing the boundaries of extreme water sports.
